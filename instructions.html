Instructions
  1. This game plays just like the game Othello, with minor exceptions.
  2. There is a black player and white player. Black moves first.
  3. A legal move is a move adjacent to the current pieces, where your piece forms a sandwich with your opponents color vertically, horizontally, and/or diagonally.
  4. The inbetween pieces are flipped over to your color. The score is based on how many of the current pieces are your own color.
  5. The next player moves (the players turn is indicated under the scorecard).
  6. If a player can't move legally, or chooses not to, they can skip there turn. If both players pass in subsequent turns, the game ends (it's an honors system to keep going).
  7. If the board fills to entirety, or step 6 occurs, the game ends and whoever has the most pieces wins. If the score is equal, it ends in a tie.
  8. Hit new game to start over. Can be done anytime in the game.
